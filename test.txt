#### 1. ArgoCD Custom Action (Lua Script)

* **File:** `bootstrap/shared/patches/argocd-cm.yaml` (Inferred context)
* **Feature:** Added a `restart` action for `batch/Job` resources in ArgoCD.
* **Logic:**
* The action is available only if the Job has the label `argocd.custom-action/retryable: "true"`.
* Because Jobs are immutable, the Lua script performs a "Deep Copy" of the failed job, cleanses specific fields (selectors, `controller-uid`, timestamps, and `resourceVersion`), and creates a **new** Job with a unique suffix (e.g., `-retry-<timestamp>`).
* This allows engineers to retry failed PostSync hooks or CronJobs directly from the ArgoCD UI without triggering a full Application Sync or manually deleting the stuck Job.



#### 2. External DNS Verification (`dev`/`stg` only)

* **File:** `apps/baseline/templates/external-dns.yaml`
* **Feature:** Implemented a verification Job that runs after the application sync (`PostSync` hook).
* **Resources Added:**
* **ServiceAccount:** `verification-{{ $app.name }}`
* **ClusterRole & Binding:** Grants `get` and `list` permissions on `pods` and `services` to the verification job.
* **Job:** A container running the image `verification-{{ $app.name }}:0.0.1`.


* **Configuration:**

* Annotated with `argocd.custom-action/retryable: "true"` to utilize the new restart feature mentioned above.
